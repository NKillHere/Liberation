# Simple Code of Conduct

> Since I have a problem against codes of conduct due to the type of people it invites, I will make this very simple. 

## Definitions.
  * By I, us or we, it refers to the creator of the project NKill, or the maintainer(s) of the project.<br/>

## Requirements.
  * In code reviews, pull requests and issues, you should not start flaming eachother.
  * You should provide constructive criticism or notify of any potential issues with the code/issue.
  * You should keep your conflict with the other in private.
## Enforcement.
  It shall be enforced by I, or a person equal to I.
## In layman's terms?
  Respect eachother in the repository, and keep conflicts out.
